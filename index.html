About:

Tor Jäger is a freelance illustrator based in Stockholm. He has studied graphic design at the Mid Sweden University and Informative Illustration at Mälardalen University. He has long experience of varius illustration jobs for clients including the Swedish Energy Agency, Wahlströms förlag, Opal förlag and Berghs förlag.

He has done several book cover illustrations and written and illustrated three books for children and young adults together with his brother Jan Jäger.
